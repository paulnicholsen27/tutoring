Jason Nicholsen, LICSW, MSW

Jason provides individual therapy, group therapy and couples counseling. He works with clients dealing with issues including anxiety, depression and relationship issues. He has a particular expertise on LGBT Issues, Aging Issues and Mood Disorders. Jason uses a wide range of modalities to address your concerns and personalize treatment to you as an individual.

Jason earned a MSW from New York University where he also received a bachelor's degree in Social Sciences. Jason has been endorsed by the National Association of Cognitive-Behavioral Therapists as a Certified Substance Abuse Therapist and certified by the Center for Financial Social Work.

Jason offers evening and Saturday appointments. To schedule an appointment please fill out our contact page or email us at jason@withinreachgroup.com
